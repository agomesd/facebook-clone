A selection of beautifully finished beach huts available to hire on the award winning beach at Wells-Next-The-Sea. Make your visit to the north Norfolk coast extra special and hire one of our delightful huts.


All the huts are fully equipped with a selection of outdoor chairs, windbreaks, beach toys and a gas stove to make yourself a cuppa, guaranteed to enhance your visit to the seaside come rain or shine...


Please note the huts are for day use only, pack up a picnic and it will make the perfect base for your beach day.
